About Talo

Talo is a personal gift tracking and wishlist app that helps you:

  • Track received and given gifts
  • Manage your wishlist
  • Remember birthdays and special occasions

Common Issues

I can't log in

Make sure you're using the correct email and password. You can also try Sign in with Apple.

My data is not syncing

Check your internet connection and restart the app.
