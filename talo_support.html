About Talo

Talo is a personal gift tracking and wishlist app designed to help you stay organized and never forget important moments.

  • Track received and given gifts
  • Manage your personal wishlist
  • Save gift ideas for each person
  • Remember birthdays and special occasions

Common Issues

I can’t log in

Make sure you're using the correct email and password. You can also try signing in with Apple.

My data is not syncing

Check your internet connection and restart the app. If the issue persists, contact support.

I lost my data

Make sure you are logged into the correct account. Your data is linked to your account, not your device.

Privacy

Your data is securely stored and only accessible to your account. We do not sell your personal data.
