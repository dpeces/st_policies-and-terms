Privacy Policy

Welcome to FormulaOneWidgets. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you provide. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

FormulaOneWidgets does not directly collect personal data. However, the app may interact with external services that do, such as:

  • Local data storage: Customized settings and preferences (dark mode, language, etc.).
  • External links: When opening links like F1.com or contribution terms, the external service may collect information according to its own privacy policy.

Use of Information

Data stored locally on your device is used exclusively for:

  • Enhancing your user experience.
  • Saving your personal preferences, such as dark mode or language.

Third-Party Links

The app includes links to external sites, such as F1.com or Wikimedia Commons. Please note that we do not control how these sites handle your information. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before interacting with them:

  • F1.com
  • Wikimedia Commons

User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access: Understand what information is stored on your device.
  • Control: Modify or delete local settings directly within the app.

Data Security

We do not collect data on external servers. All information is stored on your device. However, we recommend keeping your operating system up to date to ensure the security of your data.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy in the future to reflect changes to the app or legal requirements. You will be notified of any significant updates.
