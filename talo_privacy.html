Talo Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Talo (“the app”) helps users track gifts, manage wishlists, and remember birthdays and special occasions. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as email address and sign-in method.
  • Content you create in the app, including people, gifts, wishlist items, notes, and dates.
  • Optional photos you upload to gift or wishlist entries.
  • Optional contact-related data if you choose to use birthday import features.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the app’s features.
  • To sync your data securely across your devices.
  • To help you manage gifts, wishlists, birthdays, and related reminders.
  • To improve app reliability and performance.

Data Storage and Security

Talo uses third-party services such as Firebase to store and synchronize user data. Data is transmitted securely and protected using industry-standard security measures. Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account, subject to our configured security rules and service providers’ protections.

Contact Import

If you choose to import birthdays from your contacts, Talo will access only the data necessary to provide that feature. Contact access is optional and only used when you explicitly grant permission.

Photos

If you add photos to gifts or wishlist items, those photos are stored only to support the related app functionality.

Sharing of Information

Talo does not sell your personal information. Data may be processed by trusted service providers strictly to operate the app, such as authentication, storage, and sync services.

Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete your app content at any time.
  • You can stop using optional permissions, such as contacts access, in your device settings.
  • You can stop using the app and request deletion of your account data where applicable.

Children’s Privacy

Talo is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
